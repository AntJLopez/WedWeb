The Berkshire Outdoor Center Lodges

Tony and Haya chose this venue for their wedding because they are most excited about spending a whole weekend with everyone they love. They want to maximize time with everyone and bring their community of friends and family together. The wedding venue has four winterized lodges that can accommodate guests. Haya and Tony will be staying on-site all weekend, and encourage you to join them!

The lodge set-up and what’s included:

  • Awesome fun activities like archery, high ropes course, snow shoeing, hikes, and candle-making!
  • All rooms are equipped with twin sized beds (some are bunk beds).
  • The beds will be made with sheets, pillows, blankets, and towels.
  • There are plenty of communal bathrooms situated within each lodge.
  • Outlets and Wi-Fi are both available.
  • Heat and hot water (obviously)
  • The price includes plenty of snacks, and all your meals throughout your stay. For example, if you stay Friday through Sunday, dinner will be provided on Friday, as well as breakfast and lunch on Saturday before the wedding.
